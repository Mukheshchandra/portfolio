About Me

I’m Mukhesh Chandra Mekala, a dedicated Software Engineer and Web Developer with a strong passion for building scalable, high-performance applications. With a Master’s degree in Information Systems from Northeastern University and hands-on industry experience, I specialize in full-stack development, cloud computing, and API optimization. My expertise spans across multiple technologies, including ReactJS, NodeJS, Go, AWS, MongoDB, and MySQL.

During my tenure at Edureka and Zopsmart, I contributed to developing robust, real-time applications, optimizing system performance, and implementing scalable microservices architectures. At Edureka, I focused on architecting cross-device compatible interfaces using ReactJS and Redux while integrating secure authentication and real-time collaboration using Socket.io. At Zopsmart, I leveraged Go to build and optimize RESTful APIs, enhancing database management and performance tuning for large-scale applications.

Beyond professional experience, I have worked on diverse projects, including FITVIBE, a fitness and nutrition tracking app, and a Quarantine Management System, which streamlined record-keeping and administration. My ability to design and develop user-centric solutions stems from my keen interest in UI/UX principles and my experience with tools like Figma for prototyping.

I am always eager to stay ahead in the tech landscape by learning and implementing emerging technologies. Whether it’s designing sleek front-end interfaces or optimizing complex backend logic, I strive to build applications that are efficient, user-friendly, and impactful. Let’s collaborate and create innovative digital solutions together!
